# Data Sources

| Dataset | Source | Year / Frequency |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| County boundaries | IEBC | 2012 |
| Constituencies | IEBC | 2012 |
| Wards | IEBC via HDX (CC0) | 2012 |
| Population | KNBS 2019 Census | 2019 |
| County area (km²) | KNBS | 2019 |
| Governors | 2022 Kenya General Election results | 2022 |
| County flags | Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA) | — |
| Public universities (41) | Commission for University Education (CUE) | 2025 |
| Private universities (12) | Commission for University Education (CUE) | 2022/23 |
| TVET institutions | TVETA Kenya | 2025 |
| Ministries / Cabinet | Office of the President (president.go.ke) | 2024 |
| Parks & Reserves | Kenya Wildlife Service / beyondforest.org | 2026 |
| Presidents | Wikipedia / Kenyan constitutional records | 1963–present |
| Postal codes | Posta Kenya / postzipcode.com | — |
| Exchange rates | open.er-api.com (live mid-market) | Hourly |
| State corporations | State Corporations Act, Cap 446 / majira.co.ke | 2025 |
| Lakes | 33travels.com | 2026 |
| Rivers | abiri.home.blog | 2024 |

## Notes

- **Population:** The 2019 KNBS census is the most recent available; the next census is expected in 2029.
- **Governors:** Reflects 2022 general election results. County commissioners are not included.
- **Exchange rates:** These are mid-market rates from a public aggregator, not the official CBK indicative rates. Live rates are fetched on request and cached server-side for 1 hour.
- **Wards:** The upstream IEBC/HDX CC0 dataset is missing Nairobi, Kericho, and Bomet. Those three counties have no ward data in this API.
- **County flags:** Sourced from Wikimedia Commons under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC BY-SA). Attribution required if reused.
